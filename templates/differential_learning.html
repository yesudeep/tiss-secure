Differential Learning Fieldwork

  • Fieldwork
  • Fieldwork Organizations

  • Practice what you learn.

    The Fieldwork programme provides opportunities for students to reflect, apply and integrate acquired skills to work situations in organizations. Students are placed in four different organizations, representing different industry sectors, during the two year duration of their programme.

    The programme is overseen by the Dean and by the School Board. Currently, the school deals with more than 100 organizations with respect to fieldwork.

    The students are expected to record their learnings and to compare actual business practices with theory. Focus group discussions as well as individualized feedback sessions are conducted on a weekly basis with a faculty mentor.

    • Concurrent placement

      In the first three semesters, students attend fieldwork in three different organizations, twice a week.

    • Corporate block placement

      In the final semester, students undertake a month long block fieldwork placement in an organization

    • NGO block placement

      At the end of the fourth semester, the students work in a Non governmental organization for one month
