FacultyProfiles

The institute takes pride in its exceptional faculty who are authorities in industry research, consultancy and academics. The composition of the faculty is a healthy mix of well respected academicians and business executives. At the TISS, we boast of one of the highest faculty to student ratios in the country. The faculty has also published articles in leading academic & business journels like the South Asian Journal of Management, the Journal of Indian Psychology, the Indian Journal of Labour Economics and the Economic & Political weekly.
